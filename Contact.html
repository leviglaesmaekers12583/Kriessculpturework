Contact

A direct relationship with our customers is very important to us. That is why we are happy to answer all your (special) questions personally: by telephone, e-mail or in the store. Our guiding principle is to support you with your sculpture, whether you are a craftsman, working with chisel and beater for the first time, or have to choose from one of the many materials we offer, such as stone, wood or ceramics. So don't hesitate to ask!
